Delete Your Account - KaddaCoach

If you wish to delete your KaddaCoach account and all associated data, please follow the instructions below:

Steps to Request Account Deletion:

  1. Contact our support team at sdousman@gmail.com with the subject line: "Account Deletion Request".
  2. Include your registered email address and username in the email body.
  3. Once your request is received, we will process it within 7 business days.
  4. You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been deleted.

Data Deletion Information:

Upon account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • All personal information associated with your account.
  • Your usage history and preferences.
  • Any content or data uploaded by you.

Important: Some data may be retained for legal or security reasons as required by applicable laws. We will specify if any data needs to be retained and for how long.

If you have any questions or need further assistance, please reach out to our support team.
